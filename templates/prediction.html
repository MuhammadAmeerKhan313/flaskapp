Education Predict?

Education prediction is the process of using data analytics and machine learning techniques to forecast academic outcomes such as student performance, dropout risk, or future learning needs. By analyzing historical and real-time data like attendance, test scores, and behavior, educators can identify patterns and make informed decisions to support students, personalize learning, and improve overall educational effectiveness.
